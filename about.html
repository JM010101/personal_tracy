About Tracy R Reed

Cybersecurity Architect, Expert Witness, Board Member, Speaker, and Security/Linux Instructor teaching and mentoring the next generation of security professionals.

With 29 years of cybersecurity experience, Tracy R Reed brings extensive expertise in information security, compliance, and risk management. As a vCISO, Cybersecurity Auditor, and Architect, Tracy has helped numerous organizations strengthen their security posture.

Tracy holds multiple certifications including CISSP, ISO 27001 Lead Auditor, CMMC Lead Certified Assessor, and is an Airline Transport Pilot. As an instructor at UCSD, Tracy is committed to educating the next generation of cybersecurity professionals.
